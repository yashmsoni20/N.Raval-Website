About Me

Passionate Aerospace Engineer

I'm Neer Raval, an aerospace engineer with a passion for designing and developing innovative solutions for the aviation and space industries. With a strong background in aerodynamics, propulsion systems, and structural design, I specialize in creating efficient and sustainable aerospace technologies.

My expertise spans computational fluid dynamics (CFD), finite element analysis (FEA), and flight dynamics simulation. I'm dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in aerospace engineering while maintaining a focus on safety and efficiency.

When I'm not designing next-generation aircraft or spacecraft, I enjoy staying updated with the latest industry developments, mentoring aspiring engineers, and contributing to open-source aerospace projects.
